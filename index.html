    Services

    Valuation of Buildings

    Valuation of building or property is the method of calculating the present marketable cost of a building. Valuation of a building depends on the sort of building, its structure, durability, location, size, shape, the width of roads, frontage, types and quality of building materials used and the cost of these materials.

    Valuation of Machinery, Interiors, Furniture and Fixtures

    Machinery valuation is the Market Value, Equitable Value or Fair Value and one wants to determine the fair price to be paid, then potentially Equitable Value would be appropriate.

    Valuation for Collateral security / Home loans / construction

    The term collateral security refers to the fair market value of the assets used to secure the loan. Collateral security is typically determined by looking at the recent sale prices of similar assets or by having the asset appraised by a qualified expert.

    Visa Valuation

    Visa valuation is a formal valuation report with current fair market value on your residential properties to support and assists our application with Department of immigration and Citizenship.

    Valuation for Income tax

    Valuation of Shares and Securities in case of fresh issuance and (FMV) of the shares shall be chargeable to income tax under income from other sources

    Life of Assets

    We assign the value of an asset based on the purchase price or fair value. An asset's value can change over its lifetime through wear and tear, damage, maintenance and improvements.
